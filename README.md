#Overview:

Built an interactive dashboard to analyze e-commerce sales performance and customer purchasing behavior. The project aimed to identify top products, customer segments, and revenue drivers to support data-driven business decisions.

#Key Responsibilities:

-> Cleaned and analyzed e-commerce transactional data.
-> Studied customer behavior, purchase frequency, and product trends.
-> Designed Power BI dashboards with dynamic filters and KPIs.
-> Analyzed sales trends by category, region, and time period.
-> Identified high-value customers and repeat purchase patterns.

#Skills & Tools:

Power BI, SQL, Excel, DAX, Data Analysis

#Key Highlights from Analysis:

-> Electronics and fashion categories generated the highest revenue.
-> Repeat customers contributed significantly to total sales.
-> Seasonal sales peaks were observed during festive periods.
-> Certain customer segments showed higher average order value.
-> Regional analysis revealed untapped market opportunities.
